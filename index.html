~/about

Stephen J. Hilt is a cybersecurity expert and Senior Threat Researcher at Trend Micro, focusing on Industrial Control Systems (ICS) security and emerging cyber threats. He is a co-author of Hacking Exposed: Industrial Control Systems and a frequent speaker at security conferences including DEF CON, S4x, Black Hat, and CS3STHLM. Passionate about honeynets, radio protocol analysis, and malware tracking, he works to uncover and disrupt dangerous actors in the wild.
