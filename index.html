👨‍💻 Stephen J. Hilt is a Sr. Threat Researcher with deep focus on Industrial Control Systems (ICS) and emerging cyber threats. With a passion for honeynets, radio protocol analysis, and malware operations, he helps expose some of the most dangerous actors in the wild.
